'Daily Cafe' Menu Items

Drinks & Beverages

Latte

- milk coffee made up of one or two shots of espresso, steamed milk, and a thin layer of frothed milk as a topping

Prices:

Small (99 pesos), Medium (129 pesos), and Large (149 pesos)

Americano

- coffee drink made by diluting espresso into hot water, giving it a similar strength as ordinary brewed coffee, but a different attack and flavor from it at the same time

Prices:

Small (89 pesos), Medium (109 pesos), and Large (119 pesos)

Caramel Frappe

- coffee is blended with ice, caramel syrup, and milk to create this sweet beverage with rich caramel flavor

Prices:

Small (109 pesos), Medium (129 pesos), and Large (149 pesos)

Caramel Macchiato

- a coffee beverage with a stain of espresso on top of the milk foam

Prices:

Small (129 pesos), Medium (139 pesos), and Large (159 pesos)
